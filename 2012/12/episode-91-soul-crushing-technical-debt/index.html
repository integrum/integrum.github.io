Episode #91 – Soul Crushing Technical Debt

  • #91
  • December 6, 2012
    • Agile Weekly Podcast

Featured speakers:

Clayton Derek Jade Roy

Clayton Lengel-Zigich, Derek Neighbors, Jade Meskill and Roy van de Water discuss:

  • Staying sane in the face of massive debt
  • Bighting it off one bite at a time
  • Cutting your losses and starting over
